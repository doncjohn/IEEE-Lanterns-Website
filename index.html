About LANTERN 2.0

After going through long theory on text scripts we need to gain practical knowledge for the fulfillment of our learning, Here IEEE SAHRDAYA is hosting our Flagship event LANTERN 2.0. Lantern is all about enriching the students skill through the internship. The much-awaited hands-on workshop, practical, industrial interaction all are scheduled for young buds. Seats are limited. Register soon!.

IEEE SAHRDAYA LANTERN 1.0 2017



IEEE SB of Sahrdaya College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) team up for the IEEE SAHRDAYA LANTERN 1.0 2017 – the exclusive event for the students to be empowered with technical & creative skills with an internship.

IEEE SAHRDAYA LANTERN 1.0, was been held on june 2017 at SCET campus in Thrissur, aims to provide a platform for student engineers from colleges in kerala to show that they have what it takes to challenge the masters of the internship domains. This event had increase innovative thinking, design acumen and raw programming skills of students, as well as their mental endurance. the whole round expereience in the days filled with creativity, learning and fun.
